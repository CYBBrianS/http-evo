The Future of HTTP: HTTP/3 & QUIC

What is HTTP/3?

HTTP/3 is the next major version of the Hypertext Transfer Protocol. It builds on the QUIC transport protocol, which runs on UDP instead of TCP. This shift allows for faster, more secure connections, especially useful for modern web apps and mobile devices.

Why HTTP/3 Matters

  • Faster connection setup: Eliminates TCP and TLS handshakes
  • Reduced latency: Great for mobile networks and real-time apps
  • Built-in encryption: QUIC is encrypted by default
  • Stream independence: One stream error doesnâ€™t block others
  • More resilient to packet loss: Improves performance on poor connections
